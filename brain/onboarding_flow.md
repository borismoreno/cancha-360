# Onboarding Flow

Step 1:
SUPER_ADMIN creates academy

Step 2:
System creates invitation for DIRECTOR

Step 3:
Director accepts invitation and creates account

Step 4:
Director creates teams

Step 5:
Director invites coaches

Step 6:
Coaches manage players

Player registration methods:

1. Coach registers player manually
2. Parent registers player via link (future)
3. Director registers player

Step 7:
Parents are linked to players via PlayerGuardian

Step 8:
Coaches create trainings and evaluations

Step 9:
Parents view player progress
