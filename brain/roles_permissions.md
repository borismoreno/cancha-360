# Roles and Permissions

Global Role:

- SUPER_ADMIN
  - manages entire platform
  - creates academies
  - sends invitations to directors

Academy Roles:

- DIRECTOR
  - manages academy
  - creates teams
  - invites coaches

- COACH
  - manages teams
  - creates trainings
  - records attendance
  - evaluates players

- PARENT
  - views player progress
  - views training schedule

- PLAYER
  - optional (future use)

Notes:

- Roles are assigned via Membership
- A user can have different roles in different academies
