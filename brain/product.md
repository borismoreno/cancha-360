# Product Overview

Product Name: Cancha 360

Purpose:
A SaaS platform that provides digital infrastructure for youth football academies.

The platform allows academies to:

- manage teams and players
- organize training sessions
- track attendance
- evaluate player performance
- allow parents to monitor player development

Target users:

- Academy Directors
- Coaches
- Parents

Business model (initial phase):

- Manual sales (door-to-door)
- No online payments
- Access granted by SUPER_ADMIN

Key value proposition:
Centralize academy operations and provide visibility of player development to parents.
